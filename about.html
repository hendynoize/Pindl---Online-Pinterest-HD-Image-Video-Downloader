About Pindl

Who We Are

Pindl is a simple and user-friendly web tool designed to help users download publicly available images and videos from Pinterest. Our goal is to provide a fast, clean, and reliable experience without requiring registration or software installation.

We focus on simplicity and accessibility, making it easy for anyone to use our tool across devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make online tools accessible, efficient, and easy to use for everyone. We believe that useful tools should be simple, fast, and available without unnecessary barriers.

Pindl is built with a focus on performance and usability, ensuring a smooth experience for all users.

How It Works

Pindl processes publicly accessible Pinterest links and retrieves media directly from their original sources. We do not host or store any media files on our servers.

All downloads are handled in real-time, ensuring both speed and privacy for users.

Privacy & Responsibility

We respect user privacy. Pindl does not store personal data, download history, or media files. Users are responsible for how they use downloaded content and should respect copyright and ownership.

This tool is intended for personal and educational use only.
